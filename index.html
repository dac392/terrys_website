Made-to-Order Breakfast—Any Time of Day

At Terry’s Coffee Shop, breakfast isn’t just for the morning—it’s for whenever you’re craving it! We serve fresh, made-to-order breakfast all day long, so whether you’re in the mood for fluffy pancakes, a hearty omelet, or a crispy bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich, we’ve got you covered. Every dish is prepared with quality ingredients and served hot, just the way you like it. So no matter the time of day, you can enjoy the comfort and warmth of a delicious breakfast whenever you want it.

Free Coffee with Your Breakfast Before 11 AM

Nothing pairs better with breakfast than a hot cup of freshly brewed coffee—and at Terry’s Coffee Shop, your first cup is on us! Order any breakfast platter before 11 AM and enjoy a free small drip coffee to start your day right. Whether you’re fueling up for work or enjoying a relaxed morning, our rich, locally roasted coffee is the perfect complement to your meal. Stop by early and treat yourself to a great breakfast and a free cup of coffee—because mornings should always start with something delicious!
